## 🔥 What You Should Focus On (Small-Cap Momentum)

You want stocks that:

✅ Price: $1–$20 \[Descriptive\]

✅ Avg volume: 500k–1M+ \[Descriptive\] \n ✅ Relative volume: 2×+ \[Descriptive\] \n ✅ Premarket gap: +5% or more \[Technical : Change\] \n ✅ News catalyst today \[News\] \n ✅ ATR > 5% of price \n ✅ Float < \~50M shares (optional but powerful)

These are the names that can move 20–100% in a day.
